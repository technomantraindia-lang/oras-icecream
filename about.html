The ORAS Journey

A Tradition of Purity and Delicious Taste

Established in 2018, ORAS Ice Cream began with a core promise: to produce premium ice creams and frozen desserts using rich, high-quality ingredients, pure fresh cream, and natural fruits.

Over the years, we have grown into a widely trusted brand, operating from our state-of-the-art facility in Kota, Rajasthan. Our focus is not just creating delicious scoops, but building a robust network of successful distribution partners across regions.

We are continuously expanding our presence, offering dedicated support, high-margin categories, and advanced logistical networks to fuel our distributors' growth.

Why Partner With Us?

Built for Seamless Distribution

We equip our business partners with world-class facilities and logistical resources to ensure products are stored and delivered in perfect, ready-to-sell condition.

Advanced Cold Storage Facility

We operate advanced, multi-chamber cold storage systems at our production hub. This ensures the optimal texture, creaminess, and flavor profile of ORAS desserts are locked in immediately after production.

  • Sub-zero temperature controls (-25°C to -30°C)
  • High volumetric storage capacity for bulk stock stability
  • 24/7 power backup systems for absolute safety

Refrigerated Delivery Vehicles

To support prompt supply chains, we provide dedicated refrigerated transport vehicles. These vehicles keep our ice creams fully frozen throughout transit, preventing melt and crystal formation.

  • Specialized insulated reefers maintaining sub-zero states
  • Prompt, structured schedules for delivery commitments
  • Secure transit to minimize transit damage
