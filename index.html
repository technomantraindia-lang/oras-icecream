OUR STORY

ORAS began in 2018 with a simple dream – to craft extraordinary ice cream and frozen desserts that bring happiness to everyday moments.

From the very beginning, we've poured our hearts into using the finest ingredients, rich creams, and chocolatey indulgence to create flavours that feel joyful, comforting, and truly special.

We are expanding our network! Distributors can directly contact us to partner with ORAS and bring our premium frozen desserts to their region.
